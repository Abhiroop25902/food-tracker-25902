# Mindful Bites: Mental Health & Nutrition Tracker

**Mindful Bites** is an innovative application developed for a Google Hackathon, designed to bridge the gap between nutrition and mental well-being. By leveraging the power of Gemini 1.5 Flash and Google Cloud, the app helps users understand how their dietary choices influence their cognitive performance and emotional state in the long term.

## 🧠 Better Mental Health Through Nutrition

Most nutrition apps focus solely on weight loss or muscle gain. **Mindful Bites** focuses on the **"Second Brain"** (the gut) and how food impacts your daily life beyond just calories.

### Long-Term Benefits
*   **Sugar Crash Prevention**: Identify foods that cause insulin spikes and subsequent crashes, leading to irritability and "brain fog."
*   **Cognitive Focus**: Track which meals enhance your productivity and which ones leave you feeling lethargic or drowsy.
*   **Mood Regulation**: Discover the correlation between nutrient-dense meals and stable emotional states.
*   **Sleep Quality Optimization**: Understand how late-night habits or specific ingredients affect your circadian rhythm and sleep depth.
*   **Gut-Brain Axis Health**: Promote a microbiome-friendly diet that is scientifically linked to reduced anxiety and depression.

## 🚀 Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React 19 (Vite, TS), Tailwind v4, Lucide React, Firebase SDK.
- **Backend**: Node.js Express (TS), Firebase Admin SDK, GCP Pub/Sub SDK.
- **AI**: Gemini 1.5 Flash (via Vertex AI SDK).
- **Infrastructure**: Google Cloud Pub/Sub, Cloud Run, Firestore, Firebase Storage.

## 🛠️ How It Works
1.  **Capture**: Snap a photo of your meal.
2.  **Analyze**: Our Gemini-powered worker analyzes the image for macros and mental health dimensions.
3.  **Insights**: Receive immediate feedback on potential sugar crashes, focus impacts, and mood influences.
4.  **Track**: Observe trends over time to build a diet tailored to your mental performance and happiness.
